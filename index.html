THE VISAVA EXPERIENCE

Where Mist-Covered Mountains
Meet Fiery Local Flavours

Visava Hill Resort is not merely a restaurant; it is a peaceful culinary sanctuary cradled by the rolling green valleys of Atali, Satara.

Located a stone's throw from the world-famous Kass Flower Plateau (Maharashtra's Valley of Flowers), Visava offers a luxurious and family-friendly retreat. Here, the fresh mountain air of Satara heightens your senses, preparing you for a legendary dining experience.

We take immense pride in preserving authentic Maharashtrian culinary lineages. From the rich, fire-roasted gravies of Kolhapur to the refreshing tang of coastal solkadhi, our chefs employ age-old family recipes and fresh, locally harvested ingredients to craft dishes that celebrate Maharashtra's diverse food culture.

Scenic Valley Deck

Dine overlooking the foggy valleys of Atali, surrounded by untouched lush greenery.
